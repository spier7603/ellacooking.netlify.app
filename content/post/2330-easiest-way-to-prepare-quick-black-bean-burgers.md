##### The ingredients needed to make Black Bean Burgers:

1. Take 1 can low sodium black beans
1. Get 1/2 medium onion, grated
1. Get 2 Tbs taco seasoning
1. Get 2 tsp hot sauce
1. Prepare 1 large egg
1. Make ready 1 cup seasoned bread crumbs
1. Take 3 Tbs refined coconut oil




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Black Bean Burgers:

1. Drain but do not rinse beans In large bowl, mash with fork Add seasoning, onion, crumbs Melt coconut oil in large skillet on low heat Divide mixture into 4 and form each section into a patty (they will not shrink when cooked) Place in hot oil and cook 4-5 minutes per side.
1. Serve with condiments, cheese, and buns as you like. These will be excellent on the grill as well.
