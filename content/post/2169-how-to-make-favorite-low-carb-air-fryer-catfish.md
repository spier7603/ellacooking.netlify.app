1. Take  Egg Soak

1. Make ready  Breading (this can bread about 4 fillets)
1. Make ready 2 cups Carbquik
1. Prepare 1 tbsp salt
1. Take 1 tbsp pepper
1. Prepare  Lemon Pepper (to taste)
