##### The ingredients needed to make Baked Salmon:

1. Make ready 3 Pre-packaged Salmon Portions
1. Prepare 2 Oranges
1. Make ready 3 strands fresh Rosemary Herb
1. Take 2 Tbsp Virgin Olive Oil
1. Prepare 1 Tsp Old Bay Seasoning
1. Make ready 1 Tsp Sea Salt




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Baked Salmon:

1. In a bowl place Salmon pieces.
1. Cut oranges in halves. Squeeze 1 1/2 oranges into bowl on top of Salmon. Keep 1/2 a orange and slice into thin slices for garnish.
1. Add a small amount of Old Bay and sea salt into the bowl and rub it on both sides of the salmon.
1. Cover cookie sheet with foil and sprinkle the olive oil on top of cookie sheet
1. Place the salmon 1 inch apart from one another. Add remaining seasoning on top, lay the rosemary and orange slices on top.
1. Pre heat oven for 450° degrees and bake for 20 mins. Set the rack in the middle of the oven for best results and to give the salmon a great color.
