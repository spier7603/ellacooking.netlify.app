##### The ingredients needed to make Brussel Sprouts with bacon:

1. Take 1 lb Brussel Sprouts (halved) TIP: when buying, try to buy the smaller ones. They have a better taste. Or buy them on the stalk.
1. Prepare 2 tbsp Butter
1. Take 1/4 lb Bacon
1. Get 2 tbsp Sun dried tomatoes
1. Make ready 1 as needed Everyday seasoning from Trader Joes (sea salt,mustard seeds, black peppercorns, coriander, onion, garlic,paprika, chili pepper)




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Steps to make Brussel Sprouts with bacon:

1. Cut each strip of bacon in 1/4 in. Cook the bacon on a pan and dry on paper towels. Put aside.
1. Melt the butter in a pan and add the brussel sprouts for about 4 minutes.
1. Add the sun dried tomatoes and the seasoning for another 3-4 minutes.
1. Add the bacon to the pan and mix well and cook for 2 minutes.
1. Serve and enjoy.
