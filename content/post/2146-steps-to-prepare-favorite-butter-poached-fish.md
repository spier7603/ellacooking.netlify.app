##### The ingredients needed to make Butter Poached Fish:

1. Make ready 8 Fish Fillets, cleaned and skinned
1. Make ready  Creole Seasoning
1. Make ready  Garlic Powder
1. Get  Onion Powder
1. Prepare  Lemon Juice, from half a lemon
1. Get 5-6 Tbsps. Butter




<!--inarticleads2-->

##### Instructions to make Butter Poached Fish:

1. Season fish fillets with seasonings on both sides however much you prefer. Set aside.
1. Heat a large nonstick skillet on medium heat. Add 3 Tablespoons butter and lemon juice to pan and heat til butter is melted. Add 4-5 seasoned fillets. Cover and let Poached for bout 3-4 minutes. Flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes til fish is cooked through. Remove from pan and finish same way with the remaining fish fillets (adding butter to pan when necessary).
